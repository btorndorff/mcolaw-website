Services

Bankruptcy

Virginians are hard-working people that try to meet their obligations, but sometimes events happen such as injury, divorce or layoffs that make bankruptcy the only choice available.

We have over 25 years experience in bankruptcy practice that includes Chapter 7, Chapter 13 reorganization and Chapter 11 personal and business bankruptcies. See, In re: Townside Partners, Ltd., 125 B.R. 8 (B.C.W.D.Va.1991 (assignment of rents provision in Deed of Trust).

Although we are located in Northern Virginia, we have represented debtors and creditors in the bankruptcy courts in Alexandria, Richmond, Norfolk, Charlottesville, Harrisonburg, Lynchburg, Danville, and Roanoke.

Personal Injury Accidents

For over twenty years we have help people recover tens of thousands of dollars for injuries received from negligent or deliberate acts of businesses and other individuals. Our cases have included car accidents, defective equipment, assaults, drunk drivers, injuries in the workplace and unsafe property conditions. We assist our injured clients not only in their claim for medical expenses, pain and suffering, and permanent disability, but also work on associated property damage claims, rental cars, and lost wage claims

Immigration

Our immigration practice is centered primarily on family-based immigration across the United States and at worldwide consular posts. We provide these and other services:

  • marriage petitions and fiancee visas, single status documentation
  • immediate relatives – parents, children, brothers and sisters
  • visitor visas, student visas, religious visas, medical treatment visas
  • adjustment of status to permanent resident
  • removal of temporary conditions on permanent residence
  • petitions for citizenship/naturalization

Buisness Services

For Small Business Owners– We work to meet all of your small business needs.

Other outfits may provide the very minimum necessary to register your corporate or LLC name with the State, but leave you without the necessary filings or structure to actually do business. Our firm works with you to provide comprehensive service-

  • incorporation or LLC formation

  • federal and state tax ID nos
  • fictitious name registration
  • registered agent services
  • lease review/preparation
  • asset sales agreements & stock transfer agreements
  • business license registration
  • ABC license
  • Virginia service mark registration

Traffic/Criminal

Divorce & Separation

We do uncontested divorces in Virginia.

Our divorce services include those where a spouse is located overseas or in another state and the other spouse is residing and domiciled in Virginia.

Services include separation and property settlement agreements, preparation and filing of suit, depositions, preparing for and appearing in court for entry of the final divorce order, and other associated services.

Leasing

We provide services for commercial and residential leases. This includes lease preparation, lease negotiation and lease evictions and disputes.

Wills & Estates

We prepare non-tax wills, powers of attorney, and advance medical directives in Virginia. We also assist Executors and Administrators in probate and administration of estates.
