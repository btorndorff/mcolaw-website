About Us

Our law firm is a general practice firm with over 30 years of experience in many areas including business, leasing, immigration, traffic, divorce, bankruptcy and estates.

Mark Orndorff

A graduate of Wake Forest University School of Law and Roanoke College, he is a member of the Virginia State Bar. Chris Orndorff began his law career as an Assistant Commonwealth’s Attorney in Southwest Virginia. He later continued practice there where he gained experience in civil litigation, business law, Chapter 11 bankruptcy and other areas of practice. He is listed in the 6th edition of Who’s Who in American Law. Since 1984, he has been practicing law throughout Virginia, with appearances in immigration matters in Virginia, Texas, Missouri, North Carolina, Michigan, New Jersey, West Virginia, Maryland and other states.
